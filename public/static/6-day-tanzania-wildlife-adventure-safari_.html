6-Day Tanzania Wildlife Adventure Safari

Overview

Look no further than Ozon Light Tours for the perfect experience Safari. For those looking for a true wildlife experience, Lake Manyara National Park, Ngorongoro Crater, and Serengeti National Park are ideal options. Have you ever seen a "king of junge" lion climbing a tree? Have you ever seen all five of the Big Five in one day? If not, you're on the right path; with a bit of luck and our expertise, this Safari itinerary will be a perfect fit for you.

Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival – Transfer to Arusha

Hello! Welcome to Tanzania! Make the most of your Tanzania adventure!
When you arrive at Kilimanjaro International Airport, you will be greeted by our Safari representative and driven for approximately 1 hour to Arusha Planet Lodge, Arusha. Along the route, depending on the time you arrive, you will witness some of the residents going about their daily routines; if you spot anything fascinating, feel free to ask your guide to stop the vehicle. You will check into the lodge at a convenient time to relax, take a shower, and have dinner. Enjoy your overnight stay and prepare for your journey, which will begin the following day.

Main Destination: Kilimanjaro Airport
Accommodation: Arusha Planet Lodge
Meals & Drinks: Lunch & dinner (Breakfast not included)

Day 2: Lake Manyara National Park

The day begins with breakfast at the Lodge in Arusha, followed by a 2:30-hour drive to Lake Manyara National Park. The park is highly lovely because it is located on the Great Rift Escarpment. When you get in the park, you will be awestruck by the breathtaking scenery. Since the park is relatively small in comparison to other parks in the country, the game drive lasts few hours.
Seeing tree climbing lions in Lake Manyara is a game of chance; if you're lucky, you might spot them hanging on tree branches. Other animals you might see include elephants, giraffes, gazelles, and baboons. Around the afternoon, you will enjoy a picnic lunch near to the lake while admiring the beauty of the lake and hundreds of flamingos.
After lunch, you will go on an afternoon game drive and explore the park's other attractions, such as the hot springs and the Lake Manyara Treetop Walkway.
After game drive you will drive to the lodge in Karatu for dinner and overnight.

Main Destination: Lake Manyara National Park
Accommodation: Bougainvillea Safari Lodge
Meals & Drinks: All Meals Included

Day 3-4: Serengeti National Park

Spending two nights in the Serengeti is an incredible experience. When thinking about a Tanzania safari, Serengeti should be at the top of your list.
Your day begins with breakfast, followed by a three-hour journey to Serengeti National Park. Your Safari guide will be delighted to share his knowledge and fascinating stories about his homeland. You will also have the opportunity for an en route game drive. When you reach the Serengeti's heart, you will be driven to the Camp for a hot meal. You will relax for a few minutes before embarking on an afternoon game drive.
Serengeti is home to a variety of grazers, including wildebeest, zebra, and impala, which attracts predators. If you're lucky, you'll see predator-prey actions while on a game drive.
If you miss out on seeing large cats, predators, and their behaviors, your second day will be worth it when you marvel into the Serengeti's endless plains in quest for more animals and making your fantasies come true.

Main Destination: Central Serengeti National Park
Accommodation: Thorn Tree Camp
Meals & Drinks: All Meals Included

Day 5: Ngorongoro Crater

Enjoy your breakfast before departing the Serengeti's vast plains. After breakfast, you'll head to the Ngorongoro Crater for another thrilling adventure. The crater was formed by volcanic explosions millions of years ago. Rare remaining black rhinos are common in this area, and if you're lucky, you might see one or two. The crater is a self-sustaining ecosystem that supports hundreds of species of fauna and flora.
Today will be a lucky day for you because you will have the opportunity to search for the big five animals. Ngorongoro Crater is home to the Big 5 and other resident animals such as wildebeest and zebra. While admiring the grandeur of this amazing place, you will have a picnic lunch near the hippo pool. After lunch, you'll proceed to Lake Magadi, which is home to a large number of flamingos and other migrating birds.
The game drive will last until the evening, when you will drive to the Lodge in Karatu for dinner and overnight.

Main Destination: Ngorongoro Crater
Accommodation: Bougainvillea Safari Lodge
Meals & Drinks: All Meals Included

Day 6: Karatu to Arusha (Departure)

Congratulations for such a memorable event.
The day begins with a breakfast and then a journey to Arusha City. When you arrive in Arusha, you will have lunch at one of the city's best restaurants. Arusha is an ideal getaway; depending on your departure flight, you can choose from a number of activities available in the city, such as local home visits, market visits, and many more.
Thank you for choosing Ozon Light Tours. We hope you had a fantastic time and hope to see you again soon.
We wish you a safe return flight.

Main Destination: Arusha (City)
Accommodation: No Accommodation
Meals & Drinks: Breakfast & Lunch (Dinner not included)
