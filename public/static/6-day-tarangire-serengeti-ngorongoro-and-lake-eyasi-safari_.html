6-Day Tarangire, Serengeti, Ngorongoro&Lake Eyasi Safari

Overview

Experience the ultimate 6-day safari with Ozon Light Tour where you'll encounter incredible wildlife and stay in luxurious accommodations. Your journey will take you to Tarangire, where you'll witness herds of majestic elephants. In the Serengeti, spot the iconic big cats. Explore the crater for rare rhino sightings and head to Lake Manyara for the unique tree-climbing lions. This adventure offers a complete exploration of the Northern Circuit's renowned parks.

Itinerary

Day 1: Transfer to Tarangire National Park

Your adventure kicks off after breakfast, led by our expert safari guide who'll take you from your lodge. Get ready for an enthralling game drive in Tarangire National Park, complete with a delightful lunch from your lunch box.

As the day's game drives conclude, we'll accompany you back to your lodge. Here, you can relax, relish a delectable dinner, and settle in for a cozy overnight stay.

Tarangire National Park boasts magnificent elephant herds, iconic scattered Baobab trees, and the captivating sight of tree-climbing African Pythons. Its varied landscapes, from seasonal swamps to expansive savannas, rely on the Tarangire River as the primary water source for wildlife during the dry season. Keep an eye out for thrilling moments like lions on the prowl or leopards gracefully perched in the trees above. Get ready for an unforgettable safari experience.

Main Destination: Tarangire National Park
Accommodation: Eileen's Trees Inn
Meals & Drinks: Breakfast & Dinner (Lunch not included)

Day 2-3: Serengeti National Park

Set off for an exhilarating adventure after breakfast, as we journey to Serengeti National Park for a day filled with remarkable wildlife sightings, accompanied by delicious lunch boxes in a scenic spot within the park.

In the afternoon, the excitement continues with more thrilling game drives before we reach our camp. The following day promises an entire day dedicated to exploring the wonders of this renowned park.

Serengeti National Park is world-famous for hosting the grand spectacle of the Great Migration, featuring herds of zebras, antelopes, and wildebeests. Brace yourself to witness the fascinating circle of life, with resident predators such as leopards, cheetahs, and lions playing their crucial roles. Plus, be prepared for encounters with a variety of other captivating wildlife species.

After an action-packed day of game drives, we'll escort you to your lodge.

Main Destination: Serengeti National Park
Accommodation: Kon Tiki Serengeti Camp
Meals & Drinks: All Meals Included

Day 4: Serengeti National Park to Ngorongoro

After a hearty breakfast, our expert tour guide will pick you up, equipped with picnic lunch boxes, and embark on an exciting journey to Ngorongoro for an exhilarating game drive within the Ngorongoro Crater.

This UNESCO World Heritage site is a true African natural wonder, boasting the world's largest uninterrupted caldera. Inside, you'll encounter a year-round population of approximately 30,000 large mammals, including buffaloes, lions, elephants, cheetahs, and rhinos, as well as a rich variety of birdlife like flamingos, eagles, and buzzards. Prepare for an abundance of wildlife sightings in a short span of time.

After the game drives, we'll transport you to your lodge, where you can unwind, relish a delectable dinner, and enjoy a peaceful overnight stay. Get ready for an extraordinary safari experience in the heart of Ngorongoro.

Main Destination: Ngorongoro Crater
Accommodation: Eileen's Trees Inn
Meals & Drinks: All Meals Included

Day 5: Transfer to lake Eyasi for cultural Tour experience

After a delightful breakfast, our safari guide will whisk you away to Lake Eyasi, where you'll have the unique opportunity to meet the renowned Hadzabe community. These skilled hunters will invite you to join them on a hunting expedition, providing a firsthand glimpse into their hunting techniques.

Following your time with the Hadzabe, we'll introduce you to the Datoga community, known for their exceptional blacksmithing skills.

Additionally, you can enjoy a leisurely stroll around Lake Eyasi, explore the nearby local villages, and immerse yourself in the authentic African way of life.

After these enriching experiences, we'll return you to your lodge, where you can relax, relish a delicious dinner, and enjoy a restful overnight stay. It's a day filled with cultural immersion and exploration.

Main Destination: Lake Eyasi
Accommodation: Eileen's Trees Inn
Meals & Drinks: All Meals Included

Day 6: Lake Manyara National Park to Arusha

Following breakfast, embark on an exciting journey to Lake Manyara for a captivating game drive, complete with lunch boxes for your convenience. After concluding the game drive, we'll transport you back to Arusha, marking the end of our service.

Lake Manyara National Park, covering 330 sq. km (127 sq. miles), features a unique landscape, including a lake that can expand to 200 sq. km (77 sq. miles) during high water levels. The park is renowned for its arboreal lions, baboon colonies that line your path, and exceptional birdwatching opportunities. Look out for elephants, hippos, giraffes, zebras, baboons, warthogs, and buffalos. Don't forget to take a break at the hippo pool to stretch your legs and observe these fascinating creatures during your game drive.

Main Destination: Lake Manyara National Park
Accommodation: No Accommodation
Meals & Drinks: Breakfast & Lunch (Dinner not included)
